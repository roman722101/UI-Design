The BMW

G 310 R

BMW G 310 R is a street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 2,64,877 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 3 colours. The BMW G 310 R is powered by 313cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 33.52 bhp and a torque of 28 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, BMW G 310 R comes up with anti-locking braking system. This G 310 R bike weighs 164 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 11 liters
